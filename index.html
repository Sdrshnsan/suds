Who Am I

Hello! I'm Sudarshan Adhikari, an IT graduate working as a Network Support Assistant. My day-to-day involves keeping networks alive — from diagnosing connectivity faults to configuring routers, switches, and fiber infrastructure.

I enjoy working close to the metal: splicing fiber, configuring VLANs, writing diagnostic scripts in Python, and learning how traffic really moves through a network. I'm drawn equally to Cybersecurity — firewalls, threat detection, and building hardened systems.

Beyond the terminal, I'm curious about aviation physics and spend too many 2am hours on philosophy. I believe the best engineers are explorers first.
